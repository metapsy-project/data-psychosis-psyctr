Symptom outcomes were extracted as post-intervention mean scores with standard deviations for each study arm for total, positive, and negative symptoms. If post-intervention data were unavailable, mean change scores with corresponding standard deviations were extracted instead. 
When standard deviations were not reported, they were calculated from the standard errors, if reported.
If total PANSS scores were not reported but the PANSS positive, negative, and general subscales were available, total PANSS scores were calculated using the correlations reported in Kay et al. (1987).
