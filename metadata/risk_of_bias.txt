Risk of bias was assessed using the Cochrane Risk of Bias Tool for Randomized Trials (RoB 2; Sterne et al. 2019) across five domains: 1) randomization process, 2) deviations from intended interventions, 3) missing outcome data, 4) outcome measurement, and 5) selection of reported results. All data were rated by two independent raters (Dj.B. and A.L.) to ensure accuracy and reliability. In cases of disagreement, a third party (C.M.) was consulted to resolve any discrepancies.
